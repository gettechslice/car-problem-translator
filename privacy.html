Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

Car Problem Translator (“we”, “our”, or “the App”) is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how it is used, and your rights.

Information We Collect

  • Diagnostic Inputs: Vehicle details and symptom descriptions you provide.
  • Device Information: Basic performance and crash data.
  • Purchase Information: Subscription or one-time purchase status (handled by Apple).

Information We Do NOT Collect

  • No usernames or passwords
  • No email addresses
  • No account creation
  • No precise location data
  • No data sold or shared with third parties

Local Storage

The App stores saved vehicles, diagnosis history, and Pro status locally on your device. This information is never transmitted to us.

In-App Purchases

All payments are securely processed by the Apple App Store. We do not handle or store payment information.

Your Rights

You may delete the app at any time to remove all locally stored data.
